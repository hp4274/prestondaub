Asset-Based Lending Overview

Asset-Based Lending (ABL) enables businesses to secure financing by leveraging the value of their tangible and intangible assets â€” including accounts receivable, inventory, machinery, and real estate. Unlike traditional loans that rely heavily on credit history, ABL focuses on the quality and value of your collateral, making it an ideal solution for companies with strong asset bases but fluctuating cash flows.

Preston Daub structures ABL facilities that scale with your business. As your asset base grows, so does your borrowing capacity. Our team works closely with appraisers, auditors, and lenders to ensure your assets are properly valued and your facility is optimized for maximum capital availability.
