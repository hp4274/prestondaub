Working Capital Overview

Every business experiences fluctuations in cash flow, whether due to seasonal cycles, rapid expansion, unexpected expenses, or delayed client invoices. Partnering with a trusted financing advisor ensures you never miss a beat. Preston Daub specializes in structuring agile working capital solutions that bridge liquidity gaps and empower continuous operation.

By cutting through red tape and simplifying the underwriting process, we deliver fast, accessible capital without unnecessary delays. Our commitment is to provide customized funding structures that protect your bottom line while giving you the financial confidence to execute on urgent opportunities.
